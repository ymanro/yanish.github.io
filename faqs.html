Frequently Asked Questions

What is the warranty period for your drills?

Our drills come with a 2-year warranty covering all manufacturing defects.

How do I register my product for warranty?

You can register your product on our website by filling out the warranty registration form with your purchase details.

What should I do if my drill stops working?

If your drill stops working, please contact our customer service department. They will guide you through the troubleshooting process and arrange for repairs if necessary.

Can I purchase replacement parts for my drill?

Yes, replacement parts are available for purchase on our website under the 'Parts and Accessories' section.

Welcome to Electric Drill

Founded in 1995, Electric Drill Company began with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, innovative products to our customers.

In the early years, we focused on delivering exceptional service and building strong relationships with our clients. We started as a small local business has grown into a leading company in our industry, thanks to our dedication to excellence and customer satisfaction. As we grew, so did our product offerings and market reach. We expanded our operations and embraced new technologies, allowing us to stay ahead of the curve and continually improve our products and services.
